About

I’m Michael Rigney, a product designer focused on creating experiences made for humans, not machines. I design for how humans think and behave, rather than how they’re expected to think and behave.

When I’m not designing, I’m likely helping others build out their ideas at the creator space I co-direct, buried in yet another book, or jamming on the guitar.

If you’re interested in chatting feel free to reach out! Also check out the design adventures I’ve had so far.
